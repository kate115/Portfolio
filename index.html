Comedy Stories

I create funny comedy stories because when I was shy, I was in mmy writing camp. Then, when we had a story problem wheel, and I got the option,"Too many flies," I tried to be brave and wrote what I could call "funny," and used my courage to ask my teacher to read my story. To my surprise, everybody laughed. That is how I started my comedy story career.
